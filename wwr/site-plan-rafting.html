Purpose

We are seasoned are trained in first aid, first aid, swift water rescue, river navigation, and a variety of interpretive guiding skills. We are here to help you down the river, teach you to paddle, and help you handle every detail of your trip so you can enjoy the ride and be safe! Our tour guides and deep knowledge of each specific river and rafting routes where they take trips.

Why is white water rafting so fun? Well we can tell you! White water rafting is a unique experience, almost every part of the rafting is an adventure. The unexpected twists and turns, the wild rapids, screams of excitment! If you like an adernaline pump, or have a sense of adventure Whitewater Rafting is for you. No matter what part of the river you're on or who you're with youll experience the trip of a lifetime!
