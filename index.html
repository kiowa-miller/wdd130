Arizona

Arizona is a hot desert state, during the summers we can hit temperatures around 120 degrees fahrenheit. Some parts of Arizona do snow during the winter but the part I live in we barely have a winter. Although Arizona looks like a lot of desert, we do have some really beautiful sunsets. Arizona is best known for the Grand Canyon. The Grand Canyon is a mile deep carved by the Colorado river. The oldest rocks in the Grand Canyon are 1.8 billion years old. Interestingly enough the Grand Canyon is actually younger than the oldest rocks inside the canyon.
